Privacy Policy for Revubo

Effective Date: December 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Revubo (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a messaging automation platform that enables businesses to communicate with their customers through WhatsApp and other messaging channels.
We are committed to protecting user privacy and handling personal data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with a chatbot powered by Revubo, we may collect:

WhatsApp phone number

Messages and responses exchanged with the chatbot

Interaction metadata (date, time, message status)

The specific business you are interacting with

We only collect data that is necessary for providing our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

Respond to your messages

Provide customer support or automated assistance

Send updates, notifications, or promotional messages from the business you interacted with

Improve chatbot performance and service quality

Track orders, bookings, or interactions when applicable

4. Multi-Business Data Separation

Revubo serves multiple businesses. To protect user privacy:

Your data is only accessible to the business you interacted with

Customer data from one business is never shared with another business

Each business manages communication only with its own customers

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Data may only be shared:

With the business you interact with

When required by law or legal process

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

7. Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations.
Data may be deleted upon request, subject to business or legal requirements.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:

Request access to your data

Request correction or deletion of your data

Opt out of promotional messages at any time

Requests can be made by contacting us using the details below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Any changes will be posted at this same URL.
